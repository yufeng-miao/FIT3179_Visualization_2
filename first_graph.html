What's the most popular programming language for data scientist in Australia?

Distribution of programming language used in Australia

There are many programming languages in the world, and they are also used in various fields. Then there is a question, what's the most popular language in the world? Different people in different profession must have different opinions. Thanks to 'nomilk' on kaggle, we have a set of data reveals the preferred language for data scientist in Australia. The map shows the distribution of programming language needed in different cities, by clicking on the bar chart could filter the specific language and show its own distribution.

The heatmap displays the distribution of programming language used in different areas in Australia. Mathematics & Statistics are especially prominent in this chart, which is the where programming languages are most needed. What's more, Python, R and SQL are dominated in data science field.
